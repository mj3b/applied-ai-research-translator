# Gradual Disempowerment Human Influence Pack

This pack translates *Gradual Disempowerment: Systemic Existential Risks from Incremental AI Development* into bounded enterprise AI governance instrumentation. It treats the paper as a measurement and policy-mapping source, not as a direct deployment prohibition.

The pack's operating concept is **human influence telemetry**: identifying where AI adoption changes human decision rights, weakens feedback mechanisms, shifts accountability into another function, or makes human review ceremonial.

Current verdict: `evaluation_only`.

Primary artifacts:

- `safety_policy_intake.json`: classifies the source as structural or gradual AI risk with monitoring/evaluation boundary.
- `claims.json`: extracts five translatable claims from the paper.
- `tasks.json`: defines bounded tasks for human influence retention, local-success/systemic-drift review, cross-system burden shift, augmentation-versus-displacement classification, and translation boundary review.
- `eval_plan.json`: defines assessment questions and failure conditions.
- `decision_summary.json`: records the human-gated translation verdict.
